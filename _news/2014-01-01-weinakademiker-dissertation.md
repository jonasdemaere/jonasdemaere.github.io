Jonas De Maere completed his **Weinakademiker** degree with a dissertation on the statistical consistency of expert wine tasters — and proposed a methodology for improving evaluation reliability. The Weinakademiker diploma is awarded by the Wine Academy Austria and is one of the most rigorous wine qualifications in the world.
