Dear Reader,

It was 2009 when I first sat down to write about wine. I was not a professional critic. I was simply someone who had fallen in love with what was in the glass — and, perhaps more accurately, with everything that surrounds it. The vineyards, the people, the stories. The way a wine can carry a place and a season and a personality all at once.

I wrote sporadically over the years. Life moves on, as it tends to. Sometimes between my writings entire vintages have been made, aged, and drunk.

Today, on the 16th of March 2026, I am relaunching my blog. Not with a grand plan or an editorial calendar, but in the same spirit as always: a curiosity about wine, a desire to share what I discover, and a conviction that the best conversations happen over a good glass of wine.

What you can expect here is the same as it always was. Writing about wines that move me. About regions that deserve more attention. About the people who dedicate their lives to making something extraordinary. And yes, about the pleasure of simply drinking well.

I hope you will join me.

Warm regards,

Jonas
